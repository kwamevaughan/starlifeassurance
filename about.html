About us

StarLife Assurance Limited Company is a leading Life Assurance Company offering a wide range of need-based life assurance products designed to meet the financial security needs of Ghanaians.

We were incorporated as a limited liability company in compliance with the new Insurance Law 2006, Act 724 in October 2005, thus making us the first Life insurance company to be separated as a composite company.

StarLife Assurance has, over the years, delivered value-added services to its clients and customers making us one of the preferred Life Assurance companies in Ghana.

Our Vision

To be the pacesetters in creating wealth and a fulfilling life through personal relationships with our partners.

Our Mission

To help people identify the most secured wealth creation opportunities and provide them with unique financial security solutions.

Core Values

Integrity, Innovation, Leadership, Empathy, Passion for Excellence.
