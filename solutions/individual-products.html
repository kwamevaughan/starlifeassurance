Child Lifeline Plus

The dream of providing quality education for your children or wards is one of the most important goals of every parent or guardian. Despite life’s commitments and responsibilities, a child’s quality education cannot be compromised.

The Child Lifeline Plus is an endowment plan designed to help you begin your child or ward’s education financing now, for the future.

Main benefits

  • Maturity benefit paid at the end of the policy term
  • In the event the policyholder passes on, the beneficiaries are paid a death benefit.
  • Policy loans are accessible after the policyholder has consistently paid premiums for 24 months

Optional benefits

  • Critical Illness
  • Total and Permanent Disability
  • Retrenchment benefit with a 12-month waiver of premiums
  • Personal accident for the child under cover
  • Annual premium increments to hedge against inflation

Ultimate Protection Plus

Life is full of twists and turns. The unexpected happens, the future is unknown.​ Bringing your working life to a sudden end can introduce unforeseen challenges. Your income, which is one of your greatest financial assets is here today and can be gone tomorrow. ​It is worth considering what can replace it.​

The StarLife Ultimate Protection Plus is a unique renewable term assurance policy that pays out a lump sum as replacement income following an unfortunate and unexpected event.

Personal Pension Plan

Personal Pension Plan

Retirement is the beginning of a new exciting journey of self discovery. ​It is a time to venture into the unknown to discover your renewed sense of purpose. Thus planning is crucial and our goal is to ensure that your journey into the unknown is a smooth one.​ ​

The Personal Pension Plan is designed to provide a reliable stream of income in retirement. By partnering with us, we become part of your journey to guide and support you to achieve a life of comfort during your retirement, without the risk of outliving your pension funds.​

Main benefits

Upon retirement, the policyholder has access to:

  • Regular income until they pass on
  • Provision of income for dependents
  • In the event the policyholder passes on, the beneficiaries are paid a death benefit

In the event of death of the policyholder, the outstanding payments will be a lump sum payment to the beneficiary(ies).

Optional benefits

The policyholder can request that the beneficiary(ies) continue to receive the outstanding payments due within the guarantee Period.
